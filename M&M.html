🐾 Mango & Mocha Game

Welcome to the Mango & Mocha Game! 🎮 Inspired by the classic Google Chrome Dino game, but with a fun twist — you get to play as either Mango 🐕 (the corgi mix) or Mocha 🐕 (the chocolate lab).

The game will have you jump over obstacles and collect treats along the way. Can you beat your high score with your favorite pup? 🦴✨

Stay tuned... this game is currently in development!
