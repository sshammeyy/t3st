About Me

I am a proud first-generation college student, the first in four generations of my family to pursue higher education. My journey has taught me resilience, determination, and the importance of creating opportunities not only for myself but also as an inspiration to those around me.

I have a strong passion for technology, problem-solving, and cybersecurity, where I enjoy finding innovative ways to protect information and build secure systems. Friends and colleagues describe me as driven, approachable, and curious. I enjoy collaborating with others, sharing knowledge, and turning challenges into opportunities. My goal is to make a positive impact not just in the tech world, but in every community I am part of.

Outside of cybersecurity and IT, I enjoy spending time with my two dogs — Mocha, a chocolate Labrador, and Mango, a corgi mix. 🐶🐾 I also love gaming, and my three favorite titles are: CS:GO, Rust, and League of Legends. 🎮

Beyond gaming, I enjoy reading, hiking, learning new skills, and working on creative projects that let me combine both tech and personal interests.
