🐾 Mango & Mocha Game 🐾

Welcome to the Mango & Mocha Game! 🎮 This fun project is inspired by the classic Google Chrome Dino game, but with a twist — you get to play as either Mango 🐕 (the corgi mix) or Mocha 🐕 (the chocolate lab).

Run, jump, and collect treats while avoiding obstacles. 🦴✨ The goal is simple: survive as long as you can and beat your high score!

Game currently in development... stay tuned for updates!
